Leny Yoro is set to be announced as a new Manchester United player this week.

According to a report by David Ornstein, Leny Yoro flew from Lille to Manchester this morning to undergo medical examinations before he signs the official contract with Man United. The 18-year-old showcased his defensive skills last season with Les Dougues and now he is set to join Manchester United on a €62 million transfer, with a chance to increase to €70 million with add-ons built into the deal.

United have already agreed personal terms with the player. A 5-year contract with an option to extend for an additional year will be on the table for Yoro to sign today or tomorrow.

After securing the services of Joshua Zirkzee who became the first player to be brought in following the INEOS takeover, Yoro’s arrival sends a clear message to the fans and other Premier League clubs that The Red Devils mean business for this upcoming season.

Erik Ten Hag’s club is expected to sign another central defender as they lost Raphael Varane and Willy Kambwala after the end of the last campaign.
